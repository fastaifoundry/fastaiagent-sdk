# TechCorp — Company Policies

## Support Response Times

| Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| Urgent   | 1 hour        | 4 hours           |
| High     | 4 hours       | 1 business day    |
| Medium   | 1 business day| 3 business days   |
| Low      | 2 business days| 5 business days  |

Enterprise customers with a dedicated SLA receive guaranteed response times as specified in their contract.

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: data retained indefinitely
- Cancelled accounts: data retained for 90 days, then permanently deleted
- Traces and execution logs: configurable retention (default 90 days, Enterprise unlimited)
- Deleted items: soft-deleted for 30 days, then hard-deleted

## Security Standards

- SOC 2 Type II certified
- Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Annual penetration testing by independent third party
- GDPR compliant with DPA available on request
- Air-gapped deployment option for classified environments

## Escalation Policy

1. **Tier 1 (Support Agent)**: Handle FAQs, account issues, basic troubleshooting
2. **Tier 2 (Technical Support)**: Complex technical issues, integration problems, API debugging
3. **Tier 3 (Engineering)**: Platform bugs, performance issues, security incidents
4. **Management**: Billing disputes over $500, SLA breaches, customer complaints

All billing disputes and refund requests must be escalated to the billing team via a support ticket. Support agents should never process refunds directly.

## Acceptable Use

- Agents must not be used for generating illegal content, spam, or harassment
- Rate limits apply per plan tier to ensure fair usage
- Excessive API usage beyond plan limits will result in throttling, not service interruption
- Customers are responsible for the content generated by their agents
